Natural Advanced Search for Google™

Usage guide

Perform search

Press (ctrl) + (enter) to perform the advanced search, or click the search icon button in the popup.

Suggestions

You can show/hide autocomplete suggestions if you press (ctrl) + (space).

If the interpretation is wrong

Try to be more precise. For example, you can change

content apples from one year ago

to

content = apples and date > 2020-01-01

In some cases, text properties might be interpreted incorrectly. You can prevent this by applying quations, " ... " around the words.

books with content "my journey from one year ago"

books with content my journey from one year ago

The first example would imply that we want to find books with the content "my journey from one year ago", while the other would look for books with content "my journey" where the date of the result should at most be from one year ago

Dates

You can search by dates in multiple ways:

news about Barack Obama from 2005-05-05 to 2006-06-06

images with content cats before three years ago

restaurants near me from one year ago

Date filter does not work on image and shopping searches

Text content

You can search for what text content should contain, not contain, or might contain:

contains apple

content apples and not contains banana

contains any of apple, banana or pineapple

Text location

You search on content location:

contains apple in title

contains apple in text

contains apple in url

contains apple in links to site

News, Videos, Images, Shopping and Books

You can can be more specific about your search by writing the result type:

news about apples

videos containing apples

images containing apples

shopping content apples

apple books

File type

Filter results by filetype:

dogs filetype pdf

dogs filetype docx

Website search

You can search on specific website:

website wikipedia.com content cats

Numbers and units

You can search on number and units in ranges

ferrari from 800 to 1200 unit kg

SafeSearch™

Filter explicit results using SafeSearch:

cats safe search

Language and country

You can query by language and country. Just write the language or country somewhere in the search query:

about apples chinese simplified

about apples Sweden

or be more explicit

about apples language chinese simplified

about apples country Sweden

License

You can filter results by license. This does not work with date filters. Results are not always accurate. Examples:

about apples free to use or share

about apples free to use or share even commercially

about apples free to use share or modify

about apples commercial use
